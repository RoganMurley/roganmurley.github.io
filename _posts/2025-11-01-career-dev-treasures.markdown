A big part of my job is helping other engineers grow their careers. I’ve collected resources that have personally helped me, and helped me help others.

# Practical Career Advice

- [The Magic Loop](https://www.lennysnewsletter.com/p/the-magic-loop) by Ethan Evans
  - Build your career by making a partnership with your manager that accelerates you both. This is the framework I personally use to grow.
  - [Longer form video version.](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GB0P0_nFPTA&t=637s&pp=ygULZXRoYW4gZXZhbnM%3D)
- [Don’t call yourself a programmer](https://www.kalzumeus.com/2011/10/28/dont-call-yourself-a-programmer/) by Patrick McKenzie
  - Frame your career as solving business problems, not building software. Work in profit centers not cost centers. An oldie but a goodie.
- [Three Things Blocking Your Promotion to Staff/Principal Engineer](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=xV6j2Dxvoxw) by Steve Huynh
  - Your org, project and manager may not have the scope for you to grow (but they won’t tell you that).

# Staff+ Engineering

What does it mean to be a Staff+ Engineer, and how do you get there?

- [Staff Engineer](https://staffeng.com/book/) by Will Larson
  - Mandatory reading for any Staff+ engineer. A comprehensive overview of what it means to be Staff+, how to get there and the different [Staff archetypes](https://staffeng.com/guides/staff-archetypes/). Also available as an audiobook.
- [Why and How to be a big-tech Principal Engineer](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Xfq5iyX7BLQ) by John Miller
  - Specifically covers Principal and how it differs from Staff.

# Leadership Stories

Inspiration from others can be hugely clarifying and motivating.

- [Peterman Pod](https://www.youtube.com/@RyanLPeterman) by Ryan Peterman
  - Long form candid conversations with engineering leaders.
  - Highlights:
    - [Instagram Principal Engineer on Promotions, Breaking Prod, Tech Leading](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QUhC5BDZt-E&list=PLmvMm6hsJgCc9VPDgq8SQEFMln_azyKSG)
    - [OpenAI & Meta Distinguished Engineer (IC9) On Working With Zuck, Carmack & Career Growth](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=v2JxdjTi_1I&list=PLmvMm6hsJgCc9VPDgq8SQEFMln_azyKSG)
    - [Amazon VP On Stack Ranking & PIPs, Working With Bezos, His Promotions](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=40-ENZmqcz0&list=PLmvMm6hsJgCc9VPDgq8SQEFMln_azyKSG)
    - [Shopify Distinguished Eng (L10) on Principal+ Engineering, Career Story, Regrets](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rY44ViY45q8)
- [Staff Engineer Stories](https://staffeng.com/stories/) by Will Larson
  - Stories from the Staff Engineer book.
- [StaffEng Podcast](https://podcast.staffeng.com/) by David Noël-Romas and Alex Kessinger
  - Interviews with Staff+ engineers across the industry.

# Management

As a Staff+ engineer you should understand how your manager thinks, and be good at management-shaped work.

- [High Output Management](https://www.amazon.co.uk/High-Output-Management-Andrew-Grove/dp/0679762884) by Andy Grove
  - The canonical engineering management text, a must read.
- [An Elegant Puzzle: Systems of Engineering Management](https://press.stripe.com/an-elegant-puzzle) by Will Larson
  - The engineering management counterpart to the Staff Engineer book.

# Money

Money matters. With some minimal effort you can make a lot more of it.

- [Salary Negotiation](https://www.kalzumeus.com/2012/01/23/salary-negotiation/) by Patrick McKenzie
  - The ROI on salary negotiation is _absurd_. Think in terms of fully loaded costs and the business’ goals.
- [The Trimodal Nature of Software Engineering](https://blog.pragmaticengineer.com/software-engineering-salaries-in-the-netherlands-and-europe/) by Gergely Orosz
  - Why does the same role pay 2-4x as much as different companies? How can you earn an American tech salary while working in Europe?
